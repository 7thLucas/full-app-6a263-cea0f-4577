The Problem

Hiring decisions built on gut feelings

Most interview feedback lives in scattered notes, Slack messages, and half-remembered impressions. Without structured data, teams default to subjective hunches — leading to inconsistent evaluations, hidden bias, and slow hiring loops that lose top candidates.

The Solution

Structure, score, and compare — automatically

Interview Analyzer captures every conversation, transcribes it, and applies AI-powered analysis with customizable rubrics. Hiring managers get normalized scorecards, side-by-side comparisons, and bias indicators — turning interviews into measurable, fair, team-wide data.
