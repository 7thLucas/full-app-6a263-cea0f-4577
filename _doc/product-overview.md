# Interview Analyzer

## What It Is

Interview Analyzer is a web-based platform that helps hiring teams record, transcribe, and analyze job interviews with structured scoring and actionable insights. It transforms unstructured interview conversations into measurable, comparable candidate evaluations — reducing bias, saving time, and improving hiring quality.

## Users & Personas

- **Hiring Managers** — Review candidate scorecards, compare applicants side-by-side, and make data-informed hiring decisions from a centralized dashboard.
- **Interviewers** — Conduct interviews with guided question frameworks, capture notes in real time, and receive AI-powered feedback on their interviewing technique.
- **Recruiters / HR Coordinators** — Manage interview pipelines, schedule sessions, track candidate progress, and generate hiring analytics reports.

## Core Features

### Interview Capture & Transcription
Upload audio/video recordings or paste transcripts. The system generates clean, timestamped transcripts ready for analysis.

### AI-Powered Analysis
Automatic extraction of key topics, sentiment scoring, talk-time ratios, question coverage, and candidate response quality indicators.

### Structured Scoring & Rubrics
Customizable evaluation rubrics aligned to role competencies. Interviewers score candidates against consistent criteria; the platform aggregates scores across panelists.

### Candidate Comparison Dashboard
Side-by-side candidate comparison with normalized scores, strengths/weaknesses summaries, and recommendation signals for hiring managers.

### Interview Technique Feedback
AI-generated coaching for interviewers: question effectiveness, bias indicators, conversation balance, and areas for improvement.

### Pipeline & Scheduling
Track candidates through interview stages, coordinate schedules, send reminders, and maintain a single source of truth for the hiring workflow.

### Analytics & Reporting
Hiring funnel metrics, time-to-hire tracking, interviewer calibration reports, and diversity analytics across the interview pipeline.

## Positioning

Interview Analyzer sits at the intersection of hiring operations and conversational intelligence. It is not an ATS replacement — it is the analysis and quality layer that makes every interview more structured, fair, and data-driven.

## Brand & Tone

- **Voice**: Professional, clear, confident — like a trusted hiring advisor.
- **Aesthetic**: Clean, modern, data-rich. Balanced use of whitespace with purposeful data visualizations.
- **Primary Color**: Indigo (#4F46E5) — trust, intelligence, depth.
- **Secondary Color**: Emerald (#10B981) — growth, positive outcomes.
- **Accent Color**: Amber (#F59E0B) — highlights, attention, warmth.

## Scope

### In Scope
- Interview recording upload and transcript management
- AI-driven transcript analysis and scoring
- Customizable evaluation rubrics per role
- Candidate comparison and recommendation dashboards
- Interviewer performance feedback
- Pipeline tracking and scheduling
- Analytics and reporting dashboards
- Role-based access control (Admin, Hiring Manager, Interviewer, Recruiter)

### Out of Scope
- Full applicant tracking system (ATS) functionality
- Job board posting or application intake
- Background checks or reference management
- Payroll or onboarding workflows

## Strategic Principles

1. **Structure over subjectivity** — Every interview produces comparable, scored data — not just gut feelings.
2. **Fairness by design** — Bias detection, consistent rubrics, and calibration tools are core, not afterthoughts.
3. **Insight, not overhead** — The platform saves time; it never adds process for process's sake.
4. **Team-wide visibility** — Hiring decisions are collaborative and transparent, not siloed in individual notebooks.
